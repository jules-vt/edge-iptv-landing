Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter any problems while setting up IPTV on your iPhone or iPad:

Connection Issues:

  • Double-check your server URL, username, and password
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try switching between Wi-Fi and cellular data
  • Contact your IPTV provider to verify your account status

Playback Problems:

  • Clear the app cache in EDGE IPTV settings
  • Restart the app
  • Check if other channels work (issue might be server-specific)
  • Adjust video quality settings if buffering occurs

App Performance:

  • Ensure your iOS device has sufficient free storage
  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your iPhone or iPad if needed
  • Update EDGE IPTV to the latest version
